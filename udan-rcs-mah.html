Milestones

A first-of-its-kind initiative launching Solapur–Mumbai and Solapur–Pune routes. We implement a fully state-funded VGF model beyond the Central RCS framework to ensure sustainable operations.

State RCS Model

The Government of Maharashtra independently selects routes and airline operators. As the nodal agency, MADC ensures the State-led RCS model is implemented and successfully operationalized on the ground.

Incentives & Concessions

  • 20% VGF Reimbursement

    Direct State VGF share reimbursement to airlines.

  • Free Security & Fire Services

    Provided free of cost to RCS airports.

  • Utility Subsidies

    Power & Water supplied at 50% concessional rates.

  • Free Expansion Land

    Land for airport expansion provided free of cost.

  • Zero Charges

    No Airport Landing & Parking Charges.

  • Self Ground Handling

    Permitted by airlines for operational efficiency.
