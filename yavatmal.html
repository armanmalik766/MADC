About Yavatmal Airport

Sant Gadge Baba Yavatmal Airport is currently undergoing a major transition under the MADC to strengthen regional air connectivity and infrastructure in the Vidarbha region.

Welcome to Sant Gadge Baba Yavatmal Airport. In early January 2026, MADC formally took charge to accelerate development. Planned upgrades include runway expansion for ATR-72 aircraft and a new terminal building. Night landing facilities are being fast-tracked for 24/7 operations. Future links are planned for Mumbai, Pune, and Hyderabad.
