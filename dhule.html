About Dhule

Dhule (Gondur) Airport is operated by MADC. This airport is primarily used for non-scheduled flights of VIPs and corporates. The airport infrastructure is developed over 63 Hectares, featuring a runway of 1380 meters by 30 meters with an orientation of 09/27. It also serves as a key base for aviation training.
