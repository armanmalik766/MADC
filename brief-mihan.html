Brief About MIHAN Project
NAGPUR

The Multi Modal International Cargo Hub and Airport at Nagpur (MIHAN) is a flagship project of MADC. Spearheaded by the Hon. Chief Minister of Maharashtra, this project transforms Nagpur into the logistics capital of India.

Located at the Zero Mile center of the country, MIHAN offers unmatched connectivity via Road, Rail, and Air.
