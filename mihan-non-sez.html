NON SEZ

The land adjacent to the SEZ in MIHAN notified area is called a Non-SEZ area of MIHAN. This area is reserved for the social infrastructure like Residential complexes, Commercial buildings, Shopping centres, Warehousing Terminals, Convention centre etc Residential complexes are being developed in Non-SEZ area of MIHAN by developers like Mahindra Lifespaces and Moraj Infra.

An International School along with a state-of-the-art supply chain warehouse is already operational in MIHAN Non-SEZ area Container Corporation of India Ltd (CONCOR) is constructing the Multi-Modal Logistics Park (MMLP) along with the rail terminal on 109 acres of land to connect MIHAN with different ports in the country.

The area outside processing SEZ is about 1000 ha., wherein there Residential, Commercial, Rehabilitation of PAP’s and other uses which are supporting SEZ and incidental to the MIHAN project have been planned. In this area Residential Township, Commercial Area, Rehabilitation for PAP, 12.5% Scheme Development Plots and other facilities have been planned.

Area outside SEZ is includes area for rehabilitation and settlement of PAP’s.

The domestic tarrif area (DTA) and social, recreational & educational zones will provide world class support infrastructure that strengthens the overall MIHAN Project including SEZ & nurtures the human capital.

This zone extends value addition & works as complimentary & supplementary support to MIHAN.

Total area outside SEZ is approx. 1009.78 ha. Out of 1009.78 ha. total 146.43 ha. of area has been allotted for different use.
