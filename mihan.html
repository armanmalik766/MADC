Project Overview

MIHAN is a fully operational Multi-Product Special Economic Zone (SEZ) abutting Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar International Airport. Strategically located at the Zero Mile Centre of India, it offers Single Window Clearance, state-of-the-art ready infrastructure, and Plug & Play office spaces. The project ensures excellent Air-Road-Rail-Metro connectivity and hassle-free allotment for businesses.

Welcome to MIHAN, the Multi-modal International Hub Airport at Nagpur. This is a fully operational Multi-Product Special Economic Zone located strategically at the Zero Mile Centre of India. The total project area spans 4300 Hectares, comprising 1236 Hectares of SEZ and 1018 Hectares of Non-SEZ area. We offer Single Window Clearance, concessional power rates, and ready-to-use Plug and Play office spaces. The zone features excellent Air, Road, Rail, and Metro connectivity. Businesses here benefit from Customs Duty exemptions and zero-rated GST supplies.
