Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar International Airport Nagpur. The existing airport is being upgraded and developed for modernisation by MADC, through a JVC-MIHAN INDIA LTD, in association with Airports Authority of India. Here are the silent features: The Airport will be attached to a fully operational SEZ along with MRO facility and Logistic Park. Two parallel runways with independent operations suitable for planes of all sizes. Total area of Airport is 1350 Hectares. Existing Runway is 3200 by 45 meters, to be extended to 3600 meters. Additional Parallel Second Runway is 4,000 meters Code F Runway. Terminal Building is 3 lakh Square Meters to handle 14 million passengers per annum. Parking Bays to accommodate about 100 aircrafts at a time. Commercial area of about 100 hectares for Hotels and Shopping Complexes. Air Cargo Terminals to handle 8 lakh 70 thousand Tonnes of Cargo. Dassault Reliance, Air India MRO, and TAL have already setup operations. Ensuring Quality Airport Service In Compliance with International Standards.
