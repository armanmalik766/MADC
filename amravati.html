Project Overview

Amravati (Belora) Airport is a Code 3C Greenfield project spanning 411 Ha. Operations are scheduled to commence on 16th April 2025. The site features a 2600 Sqm terminal, a 1850m runway, and hosts South Asia’s biggest Flying Training Organization (FTO) by Air India. Night landing facilities are set to be commissioned by April 2026.

Welcome to Amravati Belora Greenfield Airport. This Code 3C airport spans 411 Hectares and is scheduled to commence operations on the 16th of April, 2025. The infrastructure includes a 1850 meter by 45 meter runway with designation 08 26. The facility features a 2600 square meter terminal building and a 23 meter high ATC tower. The apron accommodates 2 aircraft parking bays. Notably, an agreement was signed on the 18th of July, 2024, to establish South Asia’s biggest Flying Training Organization by Air India here. Work on DVOR installation is in process, and Night Landing Facilities will be commissioned by April 2026.
