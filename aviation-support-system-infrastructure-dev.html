Strategic Vision: MIHAN

Nagpur being the geographical centre of India is easily accessible by Road, Rail and Air.

With vital linkages towards America, Europe, Middle East, China, Japan and Australia, the Govt. of Maharashtra decided to develop the Multi-Modal International Hub Airport at Nagpur (MIHAN).

MADC has been appointed as the nodal agency and Special Planning Authority to develop this flagship project.

Key Aviation Partners in MIHAN SEZ

AI Engineering Services Ltd.

(Formerly Air India Ltd.) - 50 Acres Allotted

  • Developed a world-class MRO facility for Boeing Aircrafts.
  • Offered services to more than 160 Narrow Body and Wide Body Boeing Aircrafts till date.
  • Agreement with Kuwait Airline for MRO services (4 aircrafts repaired/maintained already).

Reliance Aerostructure Ltd.

Co-Developer Status - 104 Acres Allotted

  • Dassault Reliance Aerospace Ltd. (DRAL): Spread over 31 acres. Manufacturing components for Falcon 2000 Business Jet and Rafale Fighter Jet.
  • DRAL scheduled to make Falcon 2000 in flying condition by Dec 2025.
  • Thales Reliance Defence Systems Ltd (TRDS): Manufacturing Radar Systems and defence products for Ministry of Defence.

Tata Advanced Systems Ltd.

(formerly TAL Manufacturing Solutions Ltd.) - 30 Acres Allotted

  • Part of MADC vision to develop aviation/aerospace manufacturing hub.
  • Manufacturing components like Floor Beams for Boeing aircrafts.
  • Manufacturing components for Airbus and Rolls Royce.

AAR Indamer Technics Pvt. Ltd.

JV of ‘AAR’ (USA) and ‘Indamer Aviation’ (India) - Approx 29.3 Acres

  • Offering MRO to Airbus A320/A321 of Indigo Airlines and aircrafts of Go First Airlines.
  • More than 86 A320 aircrafts maintained.
  • Facility for ‘C’ Check, Environmental Controlled Hanger for Painting.
  • Developing capabilities for B737 in next year.
  • Partnership with Airbus Helicopters for rotary-wing MRO ecosystem in South Asia.
  • Facilitating engine replacement (Pratt & Whitney to CFM) for IndiGo.
