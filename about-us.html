Maharashtra Airport Development Company Limited

"Aspirations leading to innovations" "Green Goals"

We are a Government of Maharashtra undertaking entrusted with the development, operation, and maintenance of airports across the State. MADC functions as the nodal agency for the Regional Connectivity Scheme (UDAN), operating airports such as Shirdi, Amravati, and Nanded, while developing upcoming airports at Vadhwan, Gadchiroli, and Ratnagiri. As the Special Planning Authority for MIHAN, Nagpur and AASHA, Shirdi, we drive integrated aviation infrastructure, sustainable growth, and regional economic development across Maharashtra.

MADC VISION

"To develop Aviation Infrastructure in the State and world-class Multi-modal International Hub Airport along with a multi-product Special Economic Zone at Nagpur"
