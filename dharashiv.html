Welcome to Dharashiv Airport, formerly known as Osmanabad Airport. Dharashiv Airport is located in the Dharashiv district of Maharashtra and is being developed and upgraded by the Maharashtra Airport Development Company Limited, also known as MADC. The upgradation project focuses on enhancing core aviation infrastructure, including runway improvements, passenger terminal development, and the introduction of modern aviation facilities in line with current standards. The primary objective of this development is to strengthen regional air connectivity, support local economic growth, and improve accessibility to the Marathwada region. Dharashiv Airport is part of a broader initiative by MADC, alongside airports such as Baramati, Latur, and Yavatmal, to expand and modernize aviation infrastructure across Maharashtra. Once upgraded, Dharashiv Airport is expected to play a vital role in regional transportation, trade, and development.
